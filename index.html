Government Engineering College, Barton Hill

The Government Engineering College, Barton Hill, Thiruvananthapuram is a State Government funded technical Institution under the Directorate of Technical Education, Government of Kerala. Established in August 1999, this institution has 16 years of tradition in moulding a group of socially responsible Engineers. Despite being relatively young, the college has reached appreciable heights and has shown commendable performances in almost all spheres.

Travancore Hub Meet

Packed with workshops ranging from amateur to beginner to expert, THM is here to rock your techy shoes. The Travancore hub boasts of many active SBs noted at different levels in IEEE for their active volunteering and technical ability. In keeping with the motto of technical first, we aim to build your technical expertise. The two days you spend with us will give you a huge opportunity to feel the IEEE experience and give you nice technical exposure and an opportunity to network among similar professionals. So what are you waiting for? Join us for name at Government Engineering College Barton Hill on 27th-28th August for an awesome experience!

Workshops

  • BEGIN.
    A WORKSHOP ON MOBILE MAKING
    Length: 16 hours

    Embedded systems are omnipresent nowadays and mobile phone is an application of embedded systems. Mobile phone is one of the most important applications of embedded systems. Nowadays, virtually everyone carries a mobile phone, including the young children. They have become the most important item individual can own. mobile phone workshop which is the microcontroller based advanced level workshop where you will learn the art of making mobile phone using Arduino and some discrete modules .In this workshop you will not only know how a mobile is made, it will also enable you to make one for yourself; in fact after the completion of the workshop you will have a working mobile phone in your hands. The classes are handled by our expert Michael Mathews who is employed at the Kerala start-up mission. You can participate as a team of 5 members. A take away kit will be provided for a team.

  • INTER.
    A WORKSHOP ON PHONEGAP
    Length: 16 hours

    PhoneGap is a free and open source framework that allows you to create mobile apps using standardized web APIs for the platforms you care about. Phonegap is an open source development tool for building fast, easy mobile apps with Javascript. Easily create apps using the web technologies you know and love: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Just Plugin your data using into the app, using whatever available UI (iPhone/Android/Blackeberry) and you are done !! The classes are handled by experts from an Uttar Pradesh based company Innovians Technology.

  • ADV.
    A WORKSHOP ON PLC & SCADA (AUTOMATION)
    Length: 16 hours

    The main objective of this WORKSHOP is to make the aspiring engineers acquainted with the conceptual as well as practical knowledge of the Industrial Automation & latest technologies being used to achieve industrial automation. The idea of organizing this workshop is to inculcate the basic fundamentals of automation in the students and provide them with a platform to work on, In The Near Future. The Most Used Guiding Force Behind An Automated Industrial Plant Is A “Programmable Logic Controller” Generally Known As A PLC. PLCs Along With Certain Other Necessary Ingredients Like Sensors, Motors, Actuators, Valves, Conveyors, Boilers, SCADA Systems, Computers & Many More, Makes A Real Automated Manufacturing Plant.The classes are handled by experts from Innovians Technology.

  • EXPERT
    ROBOT RACING HACKATHON
    Length: 16 hours

    At this level we test the ability of young moulding engineers to design and race with their own racing robot. Participants compete as a team of five which should be rich in their knowledge of assembling, programming and tuning their own racing robot for competing each other. Hardware can be purchased from the venue with in the given budget. Expert advice would be provided during the time of assembly.

  • EXPERT
    AVION- QUADCOPTER HACKATHON
    Length: 16 hours

    At this level we test the ability of young moulding engineers to design and fly their own Quad copter. Participants compete as a team of six which should be rich in their knowledge of assembling, programming and configuring their own quadcopter to a flight. The participants could either bring their own hardwares and electronics or could be purchased from the venue which would cost within a given budget. Expert advice would be provided during the time of assembly and programming.
